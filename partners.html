How to Partner

Getting started is simple

01

Initial Contact

Reach out via our contact form or email to express interest. Tell us about your organization and populations you serve.

02

Needs Assessment

We'll schedule a meeting to understand your capacity, goals, and how we can best support your HCV initiatives.

03

Partnership Agreement

Together, we develop a plan outlining roles, responsibilities, and expected outcomes.

04

Training & Setup

Our team provides comprehensive training, supplies, and support to launch HCV services at your site.

05

Ongoing Collaboration

We maintain regular communication, ongoing support, and work together toward HCV elimination.
