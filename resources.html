Hep C Facts & Figures

One-Time Exposure

Hepatitis C is transmitted through blood-to-blood contact. One exposure can lead to lifelong infection if untreated.

Most Don't Know

Up to 44% of people living with Hep C are unaware they have it. Early detection is key to stopping its spread.

Simple Cure

Modern treatments are over 95% effective and involve taking oral medication for 8 to 12 weeks — no injections needed.

Disproportionate Impact

Indigenous people, 2SLGBTQ+ individuals, people who use drugs, and people in prison are at higher risk.

Rapid Tests

We use point-of-care tests that give accurate results in under 20 minutes — even in remote or rural communities.
