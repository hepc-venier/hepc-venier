Testing Process

Quick, confidential & effective

Point-of-Care serological assays detect HCV antibodies. Testing is provided to all high-risk patients and helps normalize the experience.

Written informed consent is obtained to ensure testing respects individual rights to confidentiality, with comprehensive pre- and post-test counselling provided.

Any patient testing positive is provided counselling and additional on-site testing to determine active viral load requiring treatment.

1. Informed Consent

Written consent with full explanation of the process and your rights.

2. Finger Prick Test

Quick antibody test using a small blood sample from your finger.

3. Results & Counselling

Receive results in 20 minutes with supportive post-test counselling.

4. Confirmatory Testing

If positive, RNA testing confirms active infection on-site.

5. Linkage to Care

Immediate connection to treatment through virtual physician consultation.
