Our Mission

We are a multidisciplinary team committed to eliminating Hepatitis C in Ontario. Our goal is to increase accessibility to testing and treatment in underrepresented communities including Indigenous peoples, people who use drugs, LGBTQ+ individuals, and residents of rural and remote regions. We use pharmacy-based interventions, mobile clinics, and educational outreach to build an inclusive health initiative from the ground up.
