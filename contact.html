FAQs

Common questions

Is testing confidential?

Yes, all testing is completely confidential. Written informed consent is obtained, and your privacy is protected throughout.

How long does testing take?

Point-of-care antibody testing provides results in approximately 20 minutes. Confirmatory RNA testing may take additional time.

Is treatment covered by OHIP?

Yes, Hepatitis C treatment is covered under Ontario's drug benefit programs. Our team can help navigate coverage options.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed for testing. Our pharmacist-led model can initiate treatment directly, or connect you with a physician virtually.
