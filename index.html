Bridging Gaps in Hepatitis C Care

Targeting underserved communities across Ontario

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between screening and treatment for Hepatitis C, focusing on marginalized populations including Indigenous peoples, rural communities, and LGBTQ+ individuals.

What We Offer

We provide point-of-care testing, pharmacist-led treatment pathways, educational materials, and downloadable resources to increase accessibility.

Join the Movement

We welcome collaboration from clinics, pharmacies, researchers, and community organizations. Help us eliminate Hep C in Ontario!
