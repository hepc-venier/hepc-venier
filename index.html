Working Toward Hepatitis C Elimination in Ontario

We aim to close the gap between testing and treatment by bringing Hep C care directly to the communities that need it most—through screening, linkage, and education.

Why We Focus on Marginalized Communities

Equity in Access
We bring screening and care to people who use drugs, Indigenous populations, and LGBTQ+ communities.

Smart Linkage
Our program connects patients directly to treatment through pharmacy-based models.

Education & Empowerment
We train clinics and communities in rapid Hep C diagnosis and care navigation.

Where We Work

Our network spans rural and urban communities, prioritizing access where it’s needed most.
